Executive summary

What this home is worth, and why

This is a genuinely unusual property for Flagler Beach: a double lot (0.344 acre) holding two separate houses — a 1,844 sq ft two-story (1983) and a 1,435 sq ft one-story (1995) — for 3,279 sq ft of combined heated area, a block and a half from the beach on the barrier island, with no HOA. Nothing on the beachside matches it exactly, so this analysis treats it the way an appraiser treats a hard-to-comp property: matched beachside sales over a 24-month window, restated to the subject with band-based adjustments, cross-checked against seven AVMs and the live competition. The result carries a wider range than a tract-home CMA would, and we say so plainly.

Methodology, sources & disclosures

How this report was built

  • Subject record: Flagler County Property Appraiser (qPublic), parcel 18-12-32-2750-00060-0040; Realist tax record. Heated area = county figure (3,279 sf across two buildings).
  • MLS: Stellar MLS (Matrix), realMLS/Flexmls including its Daytona, St. Augustine/St. Johns, Hernando, Space Coast and SEMA data-shares, and Citrus County MLS (Matrix) — all three searched live June 11, 2026 with the matched criteria shown above; every count recorded, including zeros.
  • AVMs: Zillow, Realist RealAVM and RPR RVM pulled live; Realtor.com RealEstimate, Cotality and Quantarium entered from the request; Collateral Analytics not provided.
  • Like-for-like screening: oceanfront/A1A-east, gated HOA (Sunset Inlet), mainland (Lambert Ave corridor, Reserve East, Veranda Bay, Eagle Lakes, Stillwater, John Anderson), canal/marsh-front parcels excluded by satellite map, not MLS flags.
  • Adjustments: JHT price-band values ($600K - $1M band), identical math in the table, the sliders, and the team spreadsheet. Concessions netted out (333 N 12th: $7,500).
  • Known gaps this run: MLS interior photos and the PDF source-document package (tax bill, deed, permits, iMapp) could not be saved by the automated build; comp thumbnails are parcel aerials. Subject bed count verify at listing. Photos and documents available on request.

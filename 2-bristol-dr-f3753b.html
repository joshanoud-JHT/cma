Reading the spread

The Low-3 ($366,924) reflects dated, original-condition homes; the High-3 ($557,346) reflects renovated ones. The overall average ($453,321) blends both. Because condition, not bed/bath count, is the dominant value driver in Sea Colony, the recommended price reconciles this evidence with the AVM consensus (≈$508K) and lands at $500,000 for a home in average, maintained condition.

Methodology & sources

How this was built

The subject was pulled from the Flagler County Property Appraiser. Comparable sales were searched in Stellar MLS, realMLS (Flexmls), and Citrus County MLS, screened on the satellite map for location and on the appraiser/MLS records for features, then adjusted with the Josh Hanoud Team band-based values and reduced to a $/SF range. Seven automated valuation models were pulled or supplied as an independent cross-check.

Comps were screened by map, not just MLS flags. This is an interior, non-oceanfront home in a gated beachside community; private-pool and out-of-area look-alikes were removed.

The adjustment model

Adjusting each sale to match this home

Because pool and garage were hard-matched in the search, the only live adjustment in this set is the half bath (-$3,000 against the six comps that have one). The result is the "Adjusted $" and "Adj $/SF" you see in the comp tables above, and those $/SF figures — not raw prices — drive the value summary.

Methodology, confidence and sources

How this was built

Subject verified against the St. Johns County Property Appraiser (parcel 0274310220, heated area 1,696 SF — county conditioned area, not Zillow's inflated 1,997). Comparables pulled live on June 10, 2026 from all three MLS systems Josh has access to — realMLS/Flexmls (incl. St. Augustine & St. Johns, Daytona, Hernando, Space Coast data-shares), Stellar MLS Matrix, and Citrus County MLS Matrix — with every result count recorded above, including zeros. The combined set was de-duplicated by address and parcel. Like-for-like screening kept HOA-community, non-pool, 2-car, 2009+ single-family homes and excluded premium-tier communities; no saltwater-canal or barrier-island product exists in this market area. Adjustments use the Josh Hanoud Team band-based values ($250K–$500K band), concessions netted out per closed sale. AVM panel: Realist RealAVM, RPR RVM and Zillow pulled live 6/10/26; Realtor.com RealEstimate, Cotality and Quantarium entered by the agent from the listing portal on the request form; Collateral Analytics was not provided.

Comp coordinates were geocoded (Esri World Geocoder, all 100-score matches) and each pin sanity-checked against the comp's recorded subdivision. Days on market, original/final list prices and concessions come from the MLS records themselves.

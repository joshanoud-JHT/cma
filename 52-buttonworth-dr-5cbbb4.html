Methodology, confidence and sources

How this was built

Method

  • Subject facts pulled from the Flagler County Property Appraiser record (heated area = 2,012 sq ft) and cross-checked against Zillow, Realist and RPR.
  • Waterfront status verified on the satellite map: freshwater lake (not a saltwater navigable canal), so the saltwater-canal exclusion does not apply and the lake premium is treated as modest.
  • Comps pulled live from all three MLS systems (Stellar, realMLS/Flexmls, Citrus), de-duplicated, then screened on the map for pool, HOA, oceanfront and canal before any math.
  • Seven AVMs reconciled against the matched sold/pending/active evidence and a freshwater lake/dock premium.

Confidence

  • Good on the value band: seven AVMs cluster tightly ($338k–$369.5k) and 59 matched solds frame the range.
  • Watch item: interior condition of a long-held home and the exact lake-frontage premium — both can move the final number within the stated range.

How the adjustments are calibrated

Why a feature is worth what it is worth here

A bathroom in a million-dollar canal home is worth far more than the same bathroom in a $250,000 starter home, so JHT adjustment values are banded by price point. This home's value conclusion sits in the mid-$700Ks, which places it in the $600K–$1M band: bedroom $7,000; full bath $12,000; half bath $4,000; garage bay $15,000; private pool $30,000. Pool, garage, canal, and HOA status are treated as match-first screens rather than adjustments, which is why all 19 comps already agree on those features.

Methodology, confidence and sources

How this was built

MLS coverage (all three systems searched)

  • Stellar MLS (Matrix): 17 matches (5 Active, 2 Pending, 10 Sold 180-day).
  • realMLS (Flexmls incl. Daytona/St Augustine/Space Coast data share): Subject listing + 4 canal-verified solds; 2 comps not in Stellar (1 Cloverdale Ct S, 9 Crow Ct); 2 Stellar duplicates de-duped (10 Collinson Ct = #2123361, 144 Cochise Ct = #2104628).
  • Citrus County MLS (Matrix): Searched, 0 matches.

Method

  • Like-for-like screening: every comp verified saltwater-canal, private pool, 2-car garage, non-HOA, lot under 1/4 acre, Palm Coast 32137; canal status confirmed on the satellite/GIS map, not just MLS flags.
  • Adjustments use the JHT $600K–$1M band (bedroom $7,000; full bath $12,000; half bath $4,000; garage bay $15,000; pool $30,000), selected because the value conclusion lands in the mid-$700Ks. Seller concessions are netted out before adjusting.
  • Value derived three ways: the adjusted sold average across 12 closed canal-pool sales, the closest configuration matches ($725K–$796K), and reconciliation against the 7-AVM cluster and the subject's own 2023 arm's-length purchase.

Known gaps

  • The warranty deed image was portal-gated under automation; deed data was transcribed from the Landmark Web display and the appraiser record (grantor Idzior, grantee Miller, $740,000, OR 2799/304).
  • Roof year discrepancy: the 2020 reroof permit vs the 2023 year in listing remarks; flagged for buyer verification during inspection.
